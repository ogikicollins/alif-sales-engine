## Daily Sync — 30 Minutes, Every Morning

**Format: Stand-up. No slides. No prep.**

**8:00–8:05 — Yesterday's numbers (Collins reports)**
- Emails sent / open rate / replies / positive replies
- Calls made / conversations / bookings
- Any deals that moved (stage changes in HubSpot)
- Any deals that died (reason logged)

**8:05–8:15 — Today's hot list (Collins + Kaya review together)**
- Every prospect who replied in the last 24h — what's the next action and who owns it?
- Any meetings booked today — Collins prep status?
- Any proposals outstanding — what's the follow-up?

**8:15–8:25 — Blockers (Collins flags, Kaya decides)**
- Anything Collins needs from Kaya to move a deal (pricing exception, intro, case study approval)
- Any Apex issues (access, scripts, campaign changes needed)
- Any tool problems

**8:25–8:30 — One priority (single agreement)**
- Both agree on the one thing that matters most today. Not a list. One thing.

## Weekly Sales Review — Monday, 60 Minutes

**Agenda (Collins owns and sends this in Slack by 8AM before the call):**

**0:00–0:10 — Metrics read-out**
Collins reads the scorecard. No discussion yet. Just the numbers.

**0:10–0:25 — Win/Loss debrief**
One deal won: why did it close? What should we repeat?
One deal lost: why did we lose? What do we change?
If no wins/losses: pick the most advanced open deal and stress-test it.

**0:25–0:40 — Pipeline walk**
Every deal in Discovery Scheduled or later. For each one: next action, owner, deadline. Any deal stalled >7 days gets a decision: push, nurture, or cut.

**0:40–0:50 — This week's three targets**
Collins sets three specific, measurable targets. Not "do more outreach." Targets like:
- Send 300 emails to MENA D2C founders
- Follow up with all 8 positive replies from last week
- Close the proposal sent to [Company X] by Thursday

**0:50–0:60 — Build + ops**
What automation or SOP was shipped last week? What's next?
