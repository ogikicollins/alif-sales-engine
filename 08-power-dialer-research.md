## What We Need

- Auto-dials next number the moment a call ends (no dead time between calls)
- Logs calls + dispositions directly into HubSpot automatically
- Records all calls
- Handles UAE/MENA international numbers
- UAE local number available (+971) — increases MENA answer rate by 3x
- Ideally combines call + WhatsApp in one platform

---

## Options Compared

### JustCall — **Recommended**
**Cost:** $49/user/month (Sales Pro)

**Why it wins for ALIF:**
- Native HubSpot 2-way sync — every call auto-logged, no manual entry
- UAE +971 local numbers available — answer rate goes from ~8% to ~25% for MENA cold calls
- WhatsApp integration in the same platform — one tool for call + MENA follow-up
- AI call summaries — Collins reads a 3-sentence summary instead of re-listening to recordings
- Power dialer — queue up 50 numbers, auto-dials on hang-up
- SMS from same number

**What it doesn't do well:** Real-time call coaching (Dialpad does this better, but at 2x the cost)

---

### Dialpad
**Cost:** $95/user/month (Sales plan)

**Strengths:** AI call coaching in real-time, excellent transcription, good HubSpot sync
**Weaknesses:** Limited MENA local numbers, no WhatsApp, 2x the price
**Verdict:** Right tool for a US market sales team. Not right for MENA-focused outreach at this stage.

---

### Aircall
**Cost:** $50/user/month (Essentials)

**Strengths:** Solid power dialer, good HubSpot integration, clean UI
**Weaknesses:** No WhatsApp, UAE local numbers limited, no AI summaries
**Verdict:** Good backup if JustCall has issues, but JustCall's WhatsApp integration is the differentiator.

---

### Apollo.io Dialer (already in use via Apex)
**Cost:** Included in Apollo Pro

**Reality:** It's a click-to-call tool, not a power dialer. No auto-progression, limited HubSpot sync, no MENA numbers.
**Verdict:** Keep Apollo for prospecting and list building. Not for call volume.
