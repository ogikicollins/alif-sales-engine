## The Reminder Chain (Automated)

| When | Channel | Who | Message |
|------|---------|-----|---------|
| Immediately on booking | Email (Calendly) | Auto | Confirmation + reschedule link |
| T-24 hours | Email (HubSpot) | Auto | Reminder + reschedule link |
| T-1 hour | WhatsApp (MENA) | Auto via WATI | Template below |
| T-1 hour | Email (EU/US) | Auto via HubSpot | Template below |
| T-15 min | LinkedIn DM | Manual (Collins) | "On shortly — see you in 15." |

**WATI Template (T-1 hour, MENA):**
```
Hey {{1}} — Collins from ALIF here.
Quick reminder we're speaking in an hour.
If anything's come up: {{2}} [reschedule link]
Otherwise, see you then.
```

**HubSpot Email (T-1 hour, EU/US):**
```
Subject: See you in an hour, [First Name]

Just a quick note — we're on for [time] today.

[Calendly reschedule link if anything's changed]

Collins
```

## The Recovery Protocol (When They Don't Show)

**T+0 to T+5 minutes — WhatsApp/email immediately:**

MENA (WhatsApp first):
```
Hey [First Name] — Collins from ALIF. We were on for right now — 
think something may have come up. Still free to jump on? 
Or happy to find a new slot: [Calendly link]
```

EU/US (email):
```
Subject: Missed you just now

Hey [First Name] — looks like there was a mix-up. No problem at all.

Still want to connect this week? Here's my calendar: [Calendly link]

Collins
```

**T+30 minutes — call them (Collins manually):**
Use No-Show Recovery Script from `02-cold-call-scripts.md`. If no answer, leave Voicemail 2 (Audit Hook) from `03-voicemail-sop.md`.

**T+24 hours — re-engagement email if no response:**
```
Subject: Still worth a conversation?

Hey [First Name],

We missed each other yesterday — something clearly came up.

Completely understand. Still think [specific observation about their business] is worth 
15 minutes of your time.

If timing's genuinely off, just let me know — I'll leave you alone.
If not: [Calendly link]

Collins
```

**T+7 days — if still no response:**
Tag in HubSpot: "No Show — No Response." Move to 60-day nurture. Add to LinkedIn content audience.

## Prevention: The Pre-Call Asset

The best no-show prevention is making them feel invested before the call starts.

**24 hours before the call, send (automated via HubSpot):**
```
Subject: Before we speak tomorrow, [First Name]

Hey [First Name],

Looking forward to speaking tomorrow at [time].

I'll come prepared with a few specific observations about [Company Name] — 
I'll have looked at your ads/social/website beforehand.

Nothing formal. Just want to make sure the conversation is worth your time from minute one.

See you then.

Collins
```

**Effect:** When they've received something specific and personal, the psychological cost of ghosting goes up. They feel the relationship has already started.

## MENA-Specific Rules

1. **WhatsApp is the primary reminder channel.** Not email. A WhatsApp message from a real phone number converts 3x better than an email reminder for MENA prospects.
2. **Call from a UAE +971 number.** Answer rates are 3x higher than international numbers.
3. **Don't be formal.** MENA business culture is relationship-first. "Hey [First Name]" converts better than "Dear [First Name]."
