## HOW TO USE THIS LIST

Each prospect below is:
- A **real named company and founder** confirmed through public sources
- Paired with a **specific pain signal** — something publicly known about their marketing situation
- Matched with a **hyper-personalized cold call script** written to their exact situation

For contact details (email, LinkedIn direct URL, phone): use Apollo with the search filters at the bottom of this document to match each company and pull verified contact data.

**Call approach:** Read the pain signal before you dial. Reference the specific detail in your opening line. Don't read the script word-for-word — use it as a framework and let the conversation breathe.

### Quick Personalisation Formula for Apollo Prospects

2. **Meta Ad Library** → How many ads are running? Are they consistent in style?
3. **LinkedIn** → How long has the founder been in the role? (<12 months = high urgency to prove results)

Pick **one specific detail** and open your call with it. That's what makes it hyper-personalized.

## CALL FRAMEWORK (USE FOR ALL PROSPECTS)

**Structure every call in this sequence:**

1. **Name check + permission** (5 sec)
   > "Hi [Name], this is Collins from ALIF Agency — do you have two minutes?"

2. **Specific hook** (10 sec)
   > Open with ONE specific thing you observed about their brand — Meta Ad Library, Instagram, recent news, new location. NOT a generic compliment.

3. **Pain diagnosis** (15 sec)
   > Name the specific problem you think they have based on what you saw. Don't ask if they have it — tell them what it usually means.

4. **Credibility** (10 sec)
   > One relevant case study — EEVE for beauty brands, The Padel Retreat for venues, Atelier Nova for fashion.

5. **One ask** (5 sec)
   > "Can I send you one case study?" OR "Can we find 20 minutes this week?" — never pitch the whole agency in the first call.

**Target call length:** 2–4 minutes. Get permission for a follow-up email or a discovery call. Do not pitch pricing on a cold call.
