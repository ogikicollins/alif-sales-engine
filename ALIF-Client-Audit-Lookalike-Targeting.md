## PART 2 — CROSS-CLIENT PATTERNS

### Pattern 1: The Closing Moment
Every client closed at one of five moments. These are the triggers to monitor:

| Trigger | Clients | Action |
|---------|---------|--------|
| New brand / product launch | Atelier Nova, Miznier, Rolled, Sword & Towel, Winspies, EEVE, The Padel Retreat | Outreach within 48h of launch announcement |
| Multi-location expansion | Nomads Haus, Tipsy Putt | "Your brand needs to scale with your locations" angle |
| New market entry (MENA / international) | Eventic (Dubai office), Harco (sector expansion) | MENA bridge angle |
| Rebranding / identity refresh | Nero Digital Design, Harco, Sukoon Social | "The work you do deserves a brand that matches it" |
| Endorsement / commercial activation | Sam Billings | "Your audience is worth more with the right content behind it" |

---

### Pattern 2: Universal Pain Across All Clients
Three pains appeared in every single client — regardless of industry:

1. **Creative-performance disconnect** — good product or service, weak brand presence
2. **Content quality gap** — what they were posting didn't match the quality of what they delivered
3. **No system** — posting randomly, no strategy, no production cadence

These three pains are the opening line for every ALIF outreach email.

---

### Pattern 3: Decision Maker Profile
- **95% of deals** closed with Founder / Owner / Co-Founder as the primary decision maker
- Only exception: Eventic (Founder Inge de Jong), Arc Space (Product Lead Ethan Walker — likely had Founder sign-off)
- **Key insight:** No procurement. No committee. Speed = advantage. Whoever reaches the founder first with the right message at the right moment wins.

### Pattern 5: Industries That Converted
Ranked by close rate and deal value:

1. **Wellness / Spa / Fitness** (Nomads Haus, The Padel Retreat, Sword & Towel) — fast close, recurring revenue
2. **F&B / Hospitality / Entertainment** (Rolled, Tipsy Putt, Eventic) — fast close, high content need
3. **Beauty / D2C Consumer Brands** (EEVE, Miznier) — fast close, high content + ads need
4. **Fashion / Luxury / Design** (Atelier Nova, Miznier) — fast close, brand-first

6. **Tech / SaaS / Product** (Arc Space) — moderate close speed, higher ACV
7. **Agency / Creative Services** (Nero, Sukoon Social) — moderate, project-based
8. **Industrial / B2B** (Harco) — slowest close, lowest content need, weakest fit

## PART 4 — MASTER ICP SCORING FOR LOOKALIKE TARGETING

Use this to score any inbound lead or outbound prospect. Score out of 100.

| Dimension | Weight | Score Criteria |
|-----------|--------|----------------|
| Industry fit (Segments A–F above) | 25 pts | A or B segment = 5pts; C or D = 4pts; E or F = 3pts; outside = 0pts |
| Trigger present (launch / expansion / rebrand / funding) | 25 pts | 2+ triggers = 5pts; 1 trigger = 3pts; no trigger = 1pt |
| Founder / owner is the DM | 20 pts | Confirmed + reachable = 5pts; reachable cold = 3pts; gatekeeper only = 1pt |
| Brand-performance gap visible | 20 pts | Obvious gap (DIY content, no ads, generic) = 5pts; partial gap = 3pts; solid brand = 1pt |
| Revenue / budget signal | 10 pts | Confirmed ad spend or retainer budget = 5pts; revenue signal only = 3pts; unknown = 1pt |

**Priority thresholds:**
- **90–100:** Same-day personalized outreach — this is a must-close
- **75–89:** Prioritize — outreach within 24h
- **60–74:** Semi-personalized sequence — outreach within 72h
- **Below 60:** Nurture only — do not spend manual effort
