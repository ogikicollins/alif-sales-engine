## How Collins Uses Ahrefs for Sales (Not Marketing)

Most people think Ahrefs is a marketing tool. For sales, it's a prospect intelligence weapon.

**Use 1: Pre-call research in 2 minutes**
Before any discovery call, pull the prospect's domain into Ahrefs Site Explorer.
- Organic traffic trend: is it growing or declining? Declining = pain (they need to fix something).
- Top keywords: what are they trying to rank for? This tells you their priorities.
- Backlink profile: how authoritative are they? Correlates with budget and seriousness.
- Top pages: what content drives their traffic? Helps you speak their language.

**Use 2: Competitor gap for the AI offer**
When selling the AI search positioning product:
- Pull the prospect's domain + their competitor's domain into Content Gap
- Show which topics the competitor is getting AI citations for that the prospect isn't
- This becomes the specific, data-backed opening in the email and the call

**Use 3: Prospect list building**
- Search Ahrefs Keywords Explorer for terms your ICP types ("best [service] in Dubai")
- Find which competitor sites rank for those terms
- Those competitor sites' customers are your prospects
- Export competitor backlink profiles to find industry directories and association sites — every site linking to a competitor is a warm lead source

**Use 4: Market sizing for UAE/MENA**
- Search volume data for MENA-specific terms
- Shows you how big the AI search opportunity actually is in each vertical
- Use this data in the AI offer pitch: "There are 12,000 monthly searches for '[service] Dubai' on Google — and AI now handles 20% of discovery queries in this category."

## Recommendation

**If currently on Lite: upgrade to Standard.**

**Why Standard is the right tier for Collins:**
- **Content Explorer** — lets you find every piece of content on a topic, who's sharing it, and what's getting AI citations. Critical for the AI search positioning offer.
- **2 years of historical data** — lets you show a prospect their organic traffic decline over time (visual, compelling, creates urgency)
- **20 projects** — enough to track every prospect's domain during pipeline stages
- **1,500 credits/month** — enough for daily prospect lookups without rationing

**If already on Standard: no upgrade needed yet.**

**When to go to Advanced:** When you're doing agency-level reporting for clients AND for prospecting. Not yet — Standard covers everything for the current sales stage.
